
Chapter 302 Senior Investigator
While Zhao Xusi was contemplating, Frost Sword had already arranged for the members of his guild to assemble the bamboo poles they had brought with them from the beginning, and then probe the bottom of the mud to test its depth.
At the same time, he also arranged for the combat personnel to stand guard around the area, on alert for any potential attacks that might come at any moment. It must be said that when a team has a full forty people, there will be plenty of manpower to allocate for anything they want to do.
The expanse of the muddy ground was over a hundred meters long and wide, with no foul smell, but the only thing floating on it was the freshly fallen dry leaves.
At this point, it was determined that the area of mud in front of them was less than a meter deep. The four wanderers responsible for finding the entrance to the stone chamber then took turns walking into the mud, their steps cautious, each probing the depth with a bamboo pole before taking the next step forward.
As for Zhao Xusi, who was concerned about the notes in the stone chamber, although he felt that the situation was a bit too coincidental, at this point, he certainly had no taboos and would take as much as he could get.
The only problem was that when he saw the notes later, he would definitely not be able to ask for them, otherwise no matter who saw it, he would seem to want to keep the wizard's tower to himself.
Zhao Xusi was not nervous either, since the ownership of the wizard's tower ultimately belonged to him, Frost Sword would naturally hand over the notebook to him later, otherwise it would be troublesome for Zhao Xusi to have the wizard's tower without it.
So Zhao Xusi had to ensure that they could enter the wizard's tower, otherwise he would not be able to ask Frost Sword for it no matter how many reasons he had.
At this point, Frost Sword looked at the worn and gray parchment map in his hand, constantly directing the four wanderers in the muddy ground.
